Vertical Farming

Farming sector is essential to our daily life because no matter what we need to feed our population that grows rapidly on Earth. However, farming sector produce a lot of waste and pollution that posses a danger to the environment such as excessive use of pesticide which contaminates the surrounding areas that would disrupt the local ecosystem and extensive use of land and water that threatens its surrounding nature and wildlife. Considering how important this sector for us we need to find more environmentally friendly way to feed our population. Current popular method is vertical farming, instead of growing crops horizontally we grow it vertically. This will solve land and water usage issues since it will take less land to open a farm while allowing the farmer to grow more crops and reducing water usage by watering the crops from top to bottom using the force of gravity and reusing the water from above. The usage of pesticide will also get decreased and easier to control due to smaller area to apply it. In short, vertical farming is our best choice to grow crops sustainably for us and nature until we find another more environmentally friendly way to do so.
